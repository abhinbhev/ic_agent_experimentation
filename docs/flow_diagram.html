Domain knowledge

Planner Consultant & Decision Consultant are domain-agnostic — directional only, no KPIs/dimensions.
Planner & Decision Engine are domain-grounded — given DOMAIN GUIDELINES to translate that direction into concrete KPI/dimension terms.

Brahma in Brazil had a slightly weaker Q1 2026. Brand power fell to 12.9 from 13.2 in
both Q4 2025 and Q1 2025. Critically, this was not a story of weakening brand meaning
or differentiation — both improved — but of declining salience and eroding recent
consumption, which appear to be the main performance drag.

## Recommendations
1. Prioritise salience recovery — the clearest issue is declining mental availability.
   Focus on everyday purchase occasions rather than brand-building moments.
2. Rebuild social/cultural cues — reinforce Brazilian identity, sharing, fun, and
   beer-enthusiast associations where Brahma softened most.
3. Defend frequency, not trial — trial is stable; target repeat usage through loyalty
   and regular-occasion activation.
4. Concentrate geographically — extra support for Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo; protect
   momentum in Nordeste.
5. Monitor first-choice consideration and non-consideration as leading indicators in the
   next wave.
6. Benchmark directly against Heineken on power and 4-week usage to understand the
   conversion and frequency gap.
